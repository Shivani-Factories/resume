About NM College

NM College of Commerce & Economics is one of the premier institutions in the field of commerce and economics education. Established with a vision to provide quality education and foster innovation, NM College has been a beacon of excellence for students aspiring to excel in the fields of commerce, economics, and management.

Our college is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and is accredited with an A+ grade by NAAC, reflecting our commitment to maintaining high academic standards. With a CGPA of 3.42, we are proud to be recognized as a leading institution in the country.

At NM College, we believe in empowering our students through a holistic approach to education. Our curriculum is designed to not only impart theoretical knowledge but also to provide practical insights and hands-on experience. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in commerce, economics, and management, catering to the diverse interests and career aspirations of our students.

Our faculty comprises experienced educators and industry experts who are dedicated to nurturing the intellectual and personal growth of our students. We also provide various student support services, including career counseling, placement assistance, and extracurricular activities, to ensure the overall development of our students.

Join us at NM College and embark on a journey of academic excellence and personal growth. Explore our website to learn more about our programs, admissions process, and campus life. We look forward to welcoming you to our vibrant community.
