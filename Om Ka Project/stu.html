Attendance Policy

Every Learner is expected to attend 100% lectures, practicals and tutorials conducted for every course in each Semester:

Every bonafide Learner shall ordinarily be allowed to keep terms for the given Semester in a programme of his/ her enrolment, only if he/ she fulfills at least 75% attendance as an average of total number of lectures, practicals and tutorials conducted for all the courses taken together in every Semester;

It is mandatory for every Learner to have minimum 70% attendance for each course and 75% average attendance in all the courses taken together in the programme
